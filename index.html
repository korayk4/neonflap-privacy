Privacy Policy Last updated: April 10, 2026 Terraa Studios built the Neon Flap app as a free app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app. INFORMATION WE COLLECT We collect the following information to provide leaderboard and ranking features: Username (display name) — entered by the user, stored in Firebase for the leaderboard Approximate location (country) — determined via ipapi.co based on your IP address, used for country-based leaderboard filtering Game score — stored in Firebase for the global and local leaderboards Device identifier — an anonymous Firebase UID used to associate your score with your device; not linked to your real identity or Apple ID We do not collect your name, email address, precise GPS location, or any other personally identifiable information beyond what is listed above. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES Google AdMob — advertising; may collect device identifiers for ad personalization subject to your ATT consent Google Firebase (Firestore) — stores leaderboard data (username, country, score, anonymous UID) ipapi.co — determines your country from your IP address for local leaderboard filtering; no data is stored by us from this service These third-party services have their own Privacy Policies and may collect data independently. LOCAL STORAGE Game progress such as coins, skins, and settings is stored locally on your device. Leaderboard scores and your username are also stored in Firebase as described above. IN-APP PURCHASES In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store payment information. Purchase history (e.g., whether you bought the ad-free upgrade) is tracked via your anonymous Firebase UID. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
